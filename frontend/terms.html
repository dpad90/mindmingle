Safety Guidelines

At Funky Chat, we're committed to creating a safe environment for all users. Here are some guidelines to follow:

Protecting Your Privacy

  • Never share your full name, address, phone number, or email
  • Avoid sharing social media profiles with strangers
  • Do not share financial information
  • Be cautious about sharing photos or videos

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior, please use the report function. Our moderation team will review all reports promptly.

Question Mode Safety

When using Question Mode, remember:

  • Questions should be respectful and appropriate
  • As an asker, you're responsible for the conversations your questions initiate
  • As a discusser, engage respectfully with the topics and other participants

Important Safety Reminder

Funky Chat is designed for fun, casual conversation. If you feel uncomfortable at any point:

  1. Disconnect immediately
  2. Use the report function if necessary
  3. Take a break if needed

Your wellbeing is more important than any conversation.
